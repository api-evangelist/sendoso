Sendoso describes a campaign that paired a field marketing webinar invite with themed physical gifts (baseball kits), email, and delivery notifications. Gifted contacts hit 86% attendance versus 14% for non-gifted contacts, illustrating how personalized gifting cuts through digital fatigue and drives real engagement beyond registration.
